About Lum Painting

Painting isn't just a service. It's something we take pride in.

With over 10 years of experience, brothers Garrett and Warren Lum have built Lum Painting on hard work, attention to detail, and treating every home and business as if it were their own.

We know inviting a contractor onto your property takes trust. That's why we focus on clear communication, showing up when we say we will, and delivering results that truly make a difference. Whether it's a small interior refresh or a full exterior transformation, we approach every project with the same level of care.

At the end of the day, we're not just painting properties — we're helping improve the places where people live, work, and make memories.
