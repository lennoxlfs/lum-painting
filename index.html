About Lum Painting

Painting isn't just a service. It's something we take pride in.

At Lum Painting, painting isn't just a service — it's something we take pride in. With over 10 years of experience, we've built our reputation in Northeast Ohio through hard work, attention to detail, and treating every home and business as if it were our own.

We understand that inviting a contractor onto your property requires trust. That's why we focus on clear communication, showing up when we say we will, and delivering results that truly make a difference. Whether it's a small interior refresh or a full exterior transformation, we approach every project with the same level of care.

At the end of the day, we're not just painting properties — we're helping improve the places where people live, work, and make memories.

What we do

Services that cover your whole home.

Inside or out, big job or small — we treat every project with the same care.

Interior & Exterior Painting

Walls, trim, ceilings, doors, full exteriors. Clean lines, smooth finishes, and color that holds up.

Deck Staining & Repair

Pressure Washing
