# Local SEO Site QA

Use this skill as a final check before presenting a sample site or launching a final site.

## Review categories
- first impression
- navigation clarity
- service clarity
- service area clarity
- CTA clarity
- mobile experience
- trust signals
- local relevance
- section hierarchy
- brand consistency
- polish and consistency

## SEO categories
- title / topic clarity
- internal linking opportunities
- textual clarity
- entity / business detail clarity
- structured data fit
- page experience risk
- indexing or crawl risk if known

## Output structure
1. Pass/Fail Summary
2. Critical Fixes
3. Strong Points
4. Nice-to-Have Improvements

## Rules
- focus on business value
- avoid nitpicks unless they materially impact trust, clarity, search relevance, or conversion


## Production-launch checks
Before launch, verify: page titles, meta descriptions, canonical tags when relevant, robots directives, sitemap availability, favicon, Open Graph basics, schema presence, form submission behavior, phone/mailto links, map/directions links, analytics tags, and mobile CTA visibility.
