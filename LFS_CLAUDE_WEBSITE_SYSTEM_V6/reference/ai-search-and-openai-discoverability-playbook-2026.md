# AI Search and OpenAI Discoverability Playbook (2026)

## Google AI features
Use normal SEO fundamentals first.
Google's official guidance says AI features such as AI Overviews and AI Mode use the same core eligibility and quality ideas as Search:
- the page must be crawlable and indexable
- the page must be eligible to show snippets
- the content should be helpful, people-first, and clear
- the page should have good internal links, page experience, and visible textual meaning

## Practical Google AI optimization rules
- answer the main question clearly near the top
- use direct language, not fluffy abstraction
- make services, locations, outcomes, and audiences explicit
- support important points with clean headings, FAQs, comparisons, and useful media
- keep important meaning in HTML text, not only in images or client-side widgets
- use structured data that matches visible content

## OpenAI / ChatGPT search discoverability
Use OpenAI's official crawler guidance as the baseline.
Practical rules:
- allow OAI-SearchBot unless the client wants to opt out
- do not assume GPTBot and OAI-SearchBot are the same thing
- keep robots.txt intentional and easy to audit
- track referral traffic from ChatGPT where possible
- build pages that cite-worthy search systems can understand quickly

## Practical OpenAI optimization rules
- strong page topic clarity
- clear direct answers and summaries
- useful, specific facts and service details
- clean source-like formatting where helpful
- consistent entity details across site, schema, and local business profiles
- pages that are genuinely worth citing, not thin AI filler

## Anti-spam rule
Do not mass-produce thin AI pages.
Use AI to assist with high-quality, specific, reviewed pages, not scaled low-value content.
