- business name
- live domain
- target services
- target cities / service area
- business goal for the month
- Search Console screenshots or export
- GA4 screenshots or export
- Lighthouse or PageSpeed on homepage
- Lighthouse or PageSpeed on top service page

- full Search Console export
- full GA4 landing page + key event export
- Vercel Web Analytics screenshot/export
- Vercel Speed Insights screenshot/export
- GBP notes
- Bing Webmaster notes
- list of approved pages for editing
- list of pages client does not want changed
